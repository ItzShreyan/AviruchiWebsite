About Aviruchi

Aviruchi was created to bring authentic home‑style Indian snacks, sweets and condiments directly to families in the UK.

Every product is prepared in India using traditional recipes and premium ingredients, then shipped freshly so the taste is as close to home as possible.

What we offer

  • Snacks, sweets, podis, pickles, papadums, instant mixes and more.
  • Multiple pack sizes so you can choose the right grams and portion.
  • Transparent pricing and simple online ordering.
