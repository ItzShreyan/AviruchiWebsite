Our Story

Aviruchi was born from a simple belief: the true taste of India should be experienced exactly as it was meant to be—authentic, pure, and crafted with heart.

Every one of our snacks and sweets is homemade, prepared by artisans who honour culinary traditions refined over generations. No factories. No industrial shortcuts.

Once created, our delicacies are packed carefully to preserve aroma, freshness, and flavour.

Why Choose Us?

Authenticity: Traditional, handmade methods.

Freshness: Packed and handled with care.

Ingredients: Premium ingredients for a rich, genuine taste.
