Our Story

Aviruchi was founded with a simple mission: to bring the authentic, unadulterated taste of Indian sweets to the United Kingdom.

Unlike mass-produced confectioneries, our sweets are handmade in India by traditional artisans using recipes passed down through generations. We import them fresh, ensuring that when you open a box of Aviruchi sweets, it tastes exactly as it would in a sweet shop in Mumbai or Delhi.

Why Choose Us?

  • Authenticity: No shortcuts, just traditional methods.
  • Freshness: Fast air-freight delivery ensures premium quality.
  • Ingredients: We use pure ghee, saffron, and premium nuts.
